Fly Fishing Company Database Project

In this project, I worked as part of a team to develop a comprehensive database solution for a fly fishing company. Our goal was to design and implement a fully operational database system that streamlined various business processes, including:

  • Purchase orders
  • Shipping and receiving
  • Manufacturing
  • Budgeting and cost management
  • Inventory and quantity tracking

As part of the project, we created an Entity-Relationship Diagram (ERD) to map out the database structure and relationships between entities. We also developed all the backend string connections to ensure smooth integration and functionality.

This database solution helped the company improve operational efficiency by centralizing and automating data management across multiple departments. It was a rewarding experience that honed my skills in database design, problem-solving, and teamwork.
